CP-ABE for Secure Data Sharing in Delay Tolerant Networks

International Journal of Advanced Science, Technology, Engineering and Management Sciences (IJASTEMS) 2017

This paper presents a comprehensive approach to implementing Ciphertext-Policy Attribute-Based Encryption (CP-ABE) for secure data sharing in Delay Tolerant Network environments. The research addresses critical security challenges in networks with intermittent connectivity and proposes an efficient access control mechanism.

DTN Latency & Routing Analysis: Performance Evaluation of Throw Box Deployment

International Journal of Advanced Technology in Engineering and Science (IJATEST) 2017

An in-depth analysis of latency characteristics and routing protocols in Delay Tolerant Networks with a focus on Throw Box deployment strategies. The study evaluates various performance metrics and proposes optimization techniques for improved network efficiency.

Significance of Delay Tolerant Networks in Modern Communication

International Journal of Advanced Science, Technology, Engineering and Management Sciences (IJASTEMS) 2017

This paper explores the importance and applications of Delay Tolerant Networks in contemporary communication scenarios.
